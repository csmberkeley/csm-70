\question Consider the following distribution with random variables 
Fah and Kat: 

\begin{center}
\includegraphics[width=8cm]{fah_kat.jpg}
\end{center}

Find the covariance of Fah and Kat.
\begin{solution}
E(Fah) $= 1 \cdot .3 + 2 \cdot .2 + 3 \cdot .5 = \textbf{2.2}$ \newline
E(Kat) = $1 \cdot .15 + 2 \cdot .55 + 3 \cdot .3 = \textbf{2.15}$ \newline
E(KatFah) = $1 \cdot 1 \cdot .05 +  1 \cdot 2 \cdot .25 + 2 \cdot 1 
\cdot .1 + 2 \cdot 3 \cdot .1 + 3 \cdot 2 \cdot .3 + 
3 \cdot 3 \cdot .2 = \textbf{4.95}$ \newline
cov(Kat, Fah) = $4.95 - 2.2 \cdot 2.15 = \textbf{0.22}$
\end{solution}
